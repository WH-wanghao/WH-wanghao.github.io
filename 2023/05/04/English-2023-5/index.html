The Origin of China's Youth Day

China celebreates Youth Day every May 4. It was established to honor the patriotic and anti-imperialist May Fouth Movement on May 4, 1919, when a group of Chinese students protested the weak diplomatic response of the Chinese government to the Treaty of Versailles, marking the begining of China's new democratic revolution. People expressed their anger through demonstrations and strikes. The greater movements finally pressed the government to refuse to sign the Treaty.


中国五四青年节的由来

为纪念“五四运动”，中国在每年的5月4日庆祝青年节。五四爱国运动是1919年5月4日发生的一次彻底的反对 帝国主义和封建主义的爱国运动，也是中国新民主主义革命的开始。在那个时期，一群中国学生反对当时中国政府在《凡尔赛和约》上的软弱外交。人们通过示威游行和罢工来表达愤怒。更大规模的运动最终促使政府拒绝在合约上签字。

  • strike

How Chinese Youth Commemorate the May Fourth Movement

In 1949, the new government of China declared May 4 National Youth Day to preserve and develop the spirit of patriotism and democracy of the May Fourth Movement . Since then, all kinds of commemorative activities are held annually across the country. Many young people participate in volunteering activities and social practices. Some places hold coming-of age ceremonies for young adults on Youth Day.


中国青年如何过“五四”

1949年，为了继承和发扬五四运动的爱国主义和民主精神，新中国政府正式宣布5月4日为中国青年节。此后，全国各地每年都举行各项纪念活动。许多年轻人会参加志愿活动，进行社会实践。有些地方也在青年节这天为年轻人举行成人仪式。

来自 CGTN （中国国际电视台）

生词：

  • commemorate
    • v. 纪念，用以纪念
  • patriotism
    • n. 爱国主义，爱国精神
  • democracy
    • n. 民主，民主精神

China's Mars rover finds signs of water in sand dunes

Water may be more widespread and recent on Mars than previously thought, based on observations of Martian sand dunes by China's rover. The finding highlights new, potentially fertile areas in the warmer regions of Mars where conditions might be suitable for life to exist, though more study is needed . Friday's news comes days after mission leader acknowledged that the Zhurong rover has yet to wake up since going into hibernation for the Martian winter nearly a year ago.


中国祝融号新发现，火星沙丘中近期有水存在

根据中国火星车对火星沙丘的观察，火星上的水可能比以前人们想象的分布更广泛，存在时间也更接近现在。这一发现突出了火星较温暖地区存在新的、潜在的肥沃区域，那里的条件可能适合生命的存在，尽管还需要更多的研究。周五的消息在相关负责人承认祝融号火星车在近一年前进入火星冬季休眠状态后尚未醒来的几天后发布。

来自美联社（ AP News ）

Tickets sold out, transport at maximum capacity as travel spree continues during May Day holidays

Multiple tourist spots across China have witnessed tickets being sold out and advised visitors to reschedule their plans as number from the May Day holidays in the world's second-largest economy continues to smash all expectations. On Monday, authorities in Gulangyu island, the pedestrain-only island off the coast of Xiamen, announced all the ferry tickets to the island had been sold out, suggesting tourists who have not purchased tickets to visit island on another day. In Central China's Wuhan city, the Yellow Crane Tower, the traditional Chinese tower has been at maximum receiving capacity for two days straight, roughly 40,000 people.

由于世界第二大经济体——中国的五一假期出游人数继续超出所有人的预期，中国各地多个旅游景点的门票已经售罄，景区建议游客重新安排计划。周一，鼓浪屿——厦门附近一座仅限步行的岛屿——政府宣布通往该岛的所有船票已售罄，建议未购票的游客改日登岛。在中国中部的武汉市，传统建筑黄鹤楼已经连续两天达到最大接待量，大约接待了4万名游客。

El Niño, Global Weather Pattern Tied to Intense Heat, Is Expected by Fall

Forecasters from the World Meteorological Organization are reporting increased chances that the global climate pattern known as El Niño will arrive by the end of summer. With it comes increased chances for hotter-than-normal temperatures in 2024. While there is not yet a clear picture of how strong the El Niño event will be or how long it might last, even a relatively mild one could affect precipitation and temperature patterns around the world.


将引发高温的气候模式厄尔尼诺，今秋即将到来

世界气象组织的预报员报告说，被称为厄尔尼诺的全球气候模式在今年夏末到来的可能性正在增加。随着厄尔尼诺的到来，2024年的气温很可能会比正常情况下更高。 虽然目前人们还不清楚厄尔尼诺现象会有多强，或可能持续多长时间，但即使是相对温和的厄尔尼诺现象，也可能影响世界各地的降水和气温模式。

Many Europeans want climate action – but less so if it changes their lifestyle

Many Europeans are alarmed by the climate crisis and would willingly take personal steps and back government policies to help combat it, a survey suggests – but the more a measure would change their lifestyle, the less they support it. The seven-country YouGov survey tested backing for state-level climate action, such as banning single-use plastics and scrapping fossil-fuel cars, and individual initiatives including buying only secondhand clothes and giving up meat and dairy products.


很多欧洲人渴望应对气候危机——但不希望改变生活方式

一项调查显示，许多欧洲人对气候危机感到震惊，并愿意采取个人行动、支持政府政策来帮助应对气候危机——但一项措施越是能改变他们的生活方式，他们就越不支持。 YouGov（民意与数据调查机构）在七个国家的调查中测试了人们是否支持国家层面的气候行动，例如禁止使用一次性塑料制品和废除化石燃料汽车，以及人们是否支持个人生活的倡议，包括只买二手衣服和放弃肉类、乳制品。

来自卫报（The Guardian)

  • dairy
    • n. 乳制品，乳品公司
    • adj. 乳品的

Lionel Messi suspended by Paris Saint-Germain after two-day Saudi Arabia trip

The Paris Saint-Germain forward Lionel Messi has been suspended for two weeks after an unauthorised trip for two days to Saudi Arabia and will not have his contract renewed, French sports daily L' Equipe has reported. The Argentina World Cup winner is banned from playing or training with the team, and his pay will be docked during his suspension, the report said. Messi signed an optional third-season clause in his contract when he first arrived in 2021. Club officials have decided not to activate it, the report said.

May Day holidays boost provincial economies, stimulate employment in Q2

Tourist arrivals and revenue rose in a number of Chinese provinces during the May Day holiday travel rush compared with the same period of 2019, which signals accelerating economic recovery in the coming months. Among the top travel destinations, Central China's Henan Province received 55.18 million visitors in the five-day holidays, an increase of 21.3 percent from 2019, with tourism revenue hitting 31 billion yuan ($4.48 billion), up 7.69 percent from 2019.


与2019年同期相比，中国一些省份在五一假期旅游高峰期间的游客人数和收入有所上升，这预示着未来几个月经济复苏将会加速。 在最主要的旅游目的地中，中国中部的河南省在五天假期中接待了5518万游客，比2019年增长21.3%，旅游收入达到310亿元（44.8亿美元），比2019年增长7.69%。

Loneliness poses profound public health threat, surgeon general says

Loneliness presents a profound public health threat akin to smoking and obesity, U.S. Surgeon General Vivek H. Murthy warned in an advisory issued Tuesday that aims to rally Americans to spend more time with each other in an increasingly divided and digital society. Murthy said half of U.S. adults experience loneliness, which has consequences for mental and physical health, including a greater risk of depression, anxiety — and, perhaps more surprisingly, heart disease, stroke and dementia.


美国外科医生维韦克·H·穆尔蒂在周二发布的一份咨询报告中警告说，孤独是一种对公共健康的巨大威胁，与吸烟和肥胖相似，该报告旨在呼吁美国人在一个日益分化的数字社会中，花更多的时间相互交流。 穆尔蒂说，一半的美国成年人都体验着孤独感，这会影响人们的精神和身体健康，包括使人患抑郁症、焦虑症的风险更大——也许更令人惊讶的是，孤独还会带来心脏病、中风和痴呆症的风险。

来自华盛顿邮报（The Washington Post）

King Charles III crowned with regal pomp

King Charles III was crowned Saturday at Westminster Abbey, in a ceremony steeped in ancient ritual and brimming with bling at a time when the monarchy is striving to remain relevant in a fractured modern Britain. In front of world leaders, foreign royals, dignitaries and a smattering of stars, the monarch declared, “I come not to be served but to serve,” and was presented as Britain’s “undoubted king.”


国王查尔斯三世周六在威斯敏斯特教堂加冕，仪式充满了古老的仪式感，充满了金碧辉煌，君主制努力在支离破碎的现代英国维持自己的意义。 在世界各国领导人、外国王室成员、政要和明星面前，这位君主宣布：“我不是来接受服务的，而是来服务人民的”，并被授予英国“毋庸置疑的国王”称号。

U.S. Job Growth Retains Vigor Despite Economic Worries

The labor market is still defying gravity — for now. Employers added 253,000 jobs in April on a seasonally adjusted basis, the Labor Department reported Friday, in a departure from the cooling trend that had marked the first quarter and was expected to continue. The unemployment rate was 3.4 percent, down from 3.5 percent in March, and matched the level in January, which was the lowest since 1969. Wages also popped slightly, growing 4.4 percent over the past year.


劳动力市场仍在藐视地心引力——暂时还是如此。 劳工部周五报告说，经季节性调整后，雇主在4月份增加了25.3万个工作岗位，这与第一季度的冷却趋势不同，预计这种趋势将继续下去。4月失业率为3.4%，低于3月的3.5%，与1月份的水平相当，这是自1969年以来的最低水平。工资也略有增加，在过去一年中增长了4.4%。

来自纽约时报（The New York Times）
